Digital Portfolio - Oliver Thejl Eriksen

PokeApp

Hobbyproject in collaboration with Rasmus Damgaard. PokeApp is a webapp that can calculate important data for playing competitively in the game Pokemon GO. Data is calculated with a F# script and brought dynamically to the page with JavaScript.

SideQuest

Solo hobbyproject. SideQuest is my brainchild game, which i have been working on forever. It might be too grandiose and ambitious for my abilities at the moment, but i hope i will get around to release it some sweet day.

CanninArena 2

Schoolproject in collaboration with Rasmus Damgaard and Jacob Møller. CanninArena 2 is a learning game targeted at the danish folkeskole. The game is made in Python with the Pygame module. All sprites and sound effects are originals by us.

Battleships

Schoolproject in collaboration with Rasmus Damgaard. In this version of battleships you play without visuals. All output is auditorial, exploring the possibilities for gaming as a blind person. Game is made in Python.

Hangman

Schoolproject in collaboration with Jacob Møller. A simple Hangman console game that picks a random word from a list. Made in Python.
